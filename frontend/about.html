About Us

infoPurpose of the Website

BTSMAP provides an interactive map open to everyone to view data about all types of antennas in Italy: AM radio, FM, DAB, digital TV, LTE/5G, WISP (local or Eolo), and antennas with unclassified services.

The map incorporates the official AGCOM database available here.

The map incorporates the official SIRA database of Tuscany available here.

The map incorporates the official ARIA Veneto database available here.

The map is also community-driven, meaning it is updated based on user reports.

At the moment, to contribute, contact the administrator and request an account with editor permissions. In the future, submissions will be possible directly through the GUI.
